# Pricing — POIZON SNG

## Service Model

POIZON SNG is a buyer agent (посредник/байер) for Poizon (得物), China's largest authenticated fashion marketplace. We purchase items on behalf of CIS customers who cannot access the platform directly.

## What's Included in the Service

- Price and availability verification on Poizon
- Size consultation and selection assistance
- Purchase on Poizon platform in China
- Pre-shipment photo inspection
- Shipping to customer's country with tracking number
- Support until delivery

**No fixed service fee** — the service margin is included in the exchange rate applied to the item price (CNY → local currency).

## Delivery Pricing

| Shipping Type | Transit Time | Rate |
|---------------|-------------|------|
| Air (Авиа) | 3–5 days | 225 CNY/kg |
| Express | 10–12 days | 173 CNY/kg |
| Standard | ~25 days | 77 CNY/kg |

Rates are per kilogram. Actual cost depends on weight and volume of the parcel.

## Item Pricing

Items are priced in CNY (Chinese Yuan) — same as listed on Poizon.

Examples (approximate, subject to change):
- Nike Dunk Low: 500–900 CNY
- Air Jordan 1: 700–2000 CNY
- New Balance 990: 600–1200 CNY
- Adidas Yeezy: 1000–3000 CNY
- Stone Island jacket: 3000–8000 CNY

Currency conversion (CNY → RUB/KZT/BYN/other) is done at market rate with a small service margin. Final price in local currency is confirmed before payment.

## Payment

- Accepted currencies: RUB, KZT, BYN, UZS, TJS, AMD, GEL, AZN
- Payment method: bank transfer
- Payment is agreed and confirmed in Telegram before purchase
